1.Red Dead Redemption 2

Red Dead Redemption 2 is a game developed by Rockstar Studios and published by Rockstar Games in 2019(PC).
Red Dead Redemption 2 is set in 1899 USA. Cowboys' age has gone and Lawmen has more power than ever
before. Railroads connecting the country and taking law to every town thus making it harder to be an outlaw.
In this age of changes you play as an outlaw named Arthur Morgan. Arthur is in his middle 30s and
has seen some pretty bad things happen and did some pretty bad things. The game starts at a time when you
and your gang were chased by law forces after a failed heist. You try to make your trail dissappear
by going over mountains through heavy blizzard. After some events in the early gameplay the game opens
it's doors to it's massive open world.

Red Dead Redemption 2 has the most living open world to date. Every person you see has a personality,
a job, a daily routine. They talk to each other, fight, drink, go to do their work. Some of them work
in ranches some in bars some in banks. Each and every one of them feels like they're alive. Like they
are more than 0s and 1s. The engine is also one of the most realistic engine ever. Every move,
every step has a weight and makes you feel you are in a breathing, living world.

There are more than 200 species of animals in Red Dead Redemption 2. Each one has it's own attitude each
responses differently to your actions. Also, the world itself is alive every second; a mountain lion
trying to hunt a whitetail buck, a rabbit catched by an eagle or two male bucks fighting for dominance.
The world is full of life, full of action and full of stories to discover.
To sum up, Red Dead Redemption 2 is by far the best game I've ever played in last ten years.
