2.The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is an action-rpg game developed and published by CD Projekt Red in 2015.
It is inspired from fantasy novels written by Polish writer Andrzej Sapkowski. The world of witcher
can be described as middle age with magic and monsters. Magic came to this world by conjunction of the spheres
and it created monsters. So, people needed someone to fight with these monsters. What did they do you might ask.
They took orphan boys and trained them to be warriors. They made them go through intense alchemical processes,
filled their bodies with mutagens and magic to make them more powerful than a normal human.
But the proccess was too harsh for young boys and only three out of ten was capable to live,
others died in the proccess.

They trained them to become ruthless, soulles killers and after some time they started to spit on them
and called them freaks as if it wasn't them in the first place who created this. Witchers are extremely powerful
fighters who paid in coin to do dirty work of humans. They lack of emotions as a result of the proccess.
They use two swords; one silver and for monsters, and one steel for those who are stupid enough to attack
an armed witcher. They know a lot about alchemy; they can make potions, bombs and oils to have a chance to survive
against numerous monsters and creatures they encounter.

Our main character in the game is a wolf school trained witcher Geralt of Rivia. He is the main character in
all three witcher games. The story began as Geralt is on a trail of his ex lover Yennefer of Vengerberg
to hope to find a clue about his daughter Cirilla who has the ruthless elven riders the Wild Hunt trying
to catch her. Without spoiling anything I can say that story is very powerful and can make you tear up in
some points.

Quests in the game are very well designed too. A small quest you think would end up in 10 mins can lead a tragic
story where you find darkness of human nature and realize that you're in a world full of mystery and evil.
There are some side quests better than main quests and some characters you won't forget even after the game is
finished.

The world of witcher is also full of interesting places to discover. Some sights makes you stop and just watch the game.
Listening the sound of nature, watching the huge city walls in sunset and some scenes seems like they came
out of a painting. So, to conclude The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is one of the unique experiences you might have.
