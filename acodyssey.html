5.Assassin's Creed Odyssey

Assassin's Creed Odyssey is an action rpg game developed by Ubisoft Quebec and published by Ubisoft in 2018.
Assassin's Creed is series of games which takes places in historical times of the world. American revolution, French
revolution, golden age of pirates, industrial era, renaissance and ancient ages. In the game's world people can relive
their ancestors memories through a technology called Animus. In this world some people use Animus to locate very
important and powerful artifacts of the lost civilization. Some of them make this research to secure the objects and
some of them to use them in their behalf. The people who wants to use these objects are Templars. An old order who
wants to control over the world and use these objects to make people obey them. The people who fight against the
Templars are called Assassins. A brotherhood formed thousands of years ago to fight against injustice. A brotherhood who
works in the dark to serve the light.

The game takes place in Ancient Greece in between 431â€“422 BC. The story evolves around the war between Sparta
and Athens. You play as a Sparta born mercinary who tries to get his/her life together and find a way to leave the island
he/she was living on for years and explore the world. In the game you choose who you want to play; a female protagonist
named Kassandra or a male protagonist named Alexios. The story of these two has some differences but the main story
of the game is the same.

The game's world is beautiful. It has numerous islands to explore. Old temples full of riches and mystery. The map
includes Aegean Sea. So, naval battles can happen anytime between you and pirates, between you and Spartans or Athenians
and mostly between Spartans and Athenians. As a mercinary you work for the money, you are a blade for the people.
Main quests of the game has interesting characters and stories. Characters such as Sokrates can be found in the game.
Side quests in the game at the other hand they don't all gave you some good story or enjoyable time. Some of them are
just for the experience points so you can level up. So if you want to take my advice just don't try to do every side quest
not all of them worth your time. So, to sum up besides it's flaws Assassin's Creed Odyssey is a very enjoyable game
and the world of the game is just superb looking. Assassin's Creed Odyssey is one the best games I have ever played in 2010s.
