3.Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V is an open world action game developed by Rockstar Studios and published by Rockstar Games.
Grand Theft Auto V is the second most sold game of all times with estimated 135 million copies sold.
It first released in 2013 for X360 and PS3. Then remastered for next gen consoles in 2014 and finally came to PC in 2015.
GTA V is one of the most known games of all time. From United States to Iran, from ages 5-6 to 50-60. Millions of people
heard it's name and millions played. GTA V stands as a gaming icon and even after 7 years of it's release it's still in
the top ranks of most selling titles in different platforms

The game takes us to modern Southern California style state called San Andreas. The state has long beaches, high mountains,
wild forrests, suburban neighborhoods, office districts and of course heart of the cinema world, Vinewood. Yes, it does sound
familiar. At the south end of state there lies one of the most popular city of the gaming world, Los Santos. The city is full of life.
It has different neighborhoods; Vinewood hills where rich and famous live, El Corona where poor people live and Sandy Shores
where people who likes quiet and desert life live. The city is alive and full of action 24/7.

For the first time ever in the series the protagonist is not one but three. Michael, a one successful bank robber and unsuccessful
family man. He is a dad of two and trying to enjoy retirement. He has money, house, cars and a yacht but doesn't have one thing,
happines. Franklin, a man who is in his youth and trying to make his way out of the gang wars and poverty. He lives with his aunt
tries to cam out alive from the neighborhood. Trevor, a former army pilot who served in wars and still has some trauma from those
times and living in a trailer at desert with his bussiness partners.

Quest are very well designed and uses the map in every possible way. Each one is feels different and makes you sit and play the game
for hours. Open world is also full of activities too. You can be a paparazzi and try to get shots of the celebrities. Or, you can be an
assassin shoot some high end people and make your money out of it. Or, you can just play golf, ride bikes, swim, do yoga and race in
high speeds. You can do whatever you want as much as the game allows you. So, simply take your car, turn on the Non Stop Pop FM
and ride to sunset by the sea or just do a massacare. This is GTA V.
