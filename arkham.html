4.Batman: Arkham Knight

Batman: Arkham Knight is an open world action game developed by Rocksteady Studios and published by Warner Bros.
Interactive Entertainment in 2015. It is the third game of the Arkham Trilogy. The game takes place in a fictional city called Gotham.
Gotham is a city full of problems; most of the citizens live in poverty line, streets are full with homeless people and most importantly
it is ruled by gangs and criminals. Yes, there are politicians who represents the city and yes there are people who serves under the law
and rule the city. But, anyone can be bought even the ones who took an oath to serve and protect you. However there is someone who
only works for justice, someone who came out at night, a caped crusader, a detective, a billionare...
The Dark Knight of the Gotham City, Batman.

Batman is a character who is way older than most of us. It first came out in Detective Comics 27th issue in May 1939. Has seen a world
war, space race, fall of the USSR and Covid-19. It is a character who is known for generations. He was born in a very rich family and
lived his life in happines until a dreadful night at the Gotham City Opera House. Both of his parents were murdered that day for only few bucks.
After that he was never the same. He was raised by family butler Alfred Pennyworth. In time he realized something that the city needed
someone. He realized if someone was there at that night at the opera, his parents would be alive. He promised himself that he will become the
justice that city needs. He used his wealth to the benefit of the city. He used it to make himself powerfull so that people can fear him. Mafia
bosses start to make their meetings in day light, drug dealers wasn't working after dark and when people see that white light in the clouds they
knew someone is at the roofs, watching, protecting.

In Arkham Knight you play as Batman. The game starts with a nice restaurant. An officer drinking his evening coffee while reading some news
then a citizen came in and says there is someone at the back smoking in closed area which is prohibited in Gotham. So, officer walks to the stranger
and tells him to put out the cigarette. Then hell happens. People start to attack each other, plates start to fly in the air and chaos everywhere.
The interesting thing that people didn't know about this incident was, it wasn't just couple of people fighting. It was the result of the very small
amount of toxic gas that released in to the restaurant by Scarecrow, a villain that Batman had been dealing with for a long time. After the incident at the
restaurant Scarecrow released a video about the things he can do to the city with his new formula and warned the citizens of Gotham City. After the video
police start to evacuate the city. Within couple of hours city streets became soulles except the people who enjoyes this caos; criminals.

Your duty as the Batman is to find Scarecrow, end this caos and solve the mystery about the new guy named Arkham Knight who works for Scarecrow.
The game gives you couple of gadgets and also first time in the series the Batmobile. Batmobile is a vehicle which is technically a tank. It can resist
extreme amount of pressure and heat. Gameplay of the game is just beautiful. Roaming in the city with the Batmobile and bolting into the air like a bullet
from the car seamlessly. Fighting mechanics are also very good and makes you feel like Batman. Gliding between buildings, trying to solve Riddler's puzzles,
roaming in the streets, kicking some bad guys' asses. Batman Arkham Knight is one of the best games I have ever played in last decade. If you are a fan of
Batman give it a shot, it's worth it.
